Understanding Agentic AI

Agentic AI represents a paradigm shift in artificial intelligence—moving beyond reactive systems to autonomous, goal-oriented agents that can perceive their environment, make decisions, and take actions with minimal human intervention.

What are AI Agents?

AI agents are intelligent systems capable of:

  • Autonomous Decision Making: Making intelligent choices based on current state and objectives without explicit instructions
  • Multi-Step Planning: Breaking down complex problems into actionable steps and executing them sequentially
  • Environmental Adaptation: Learning from feedback and adjusting strategies based on outcomes
  • Tool Integration: Leveraging external APIs, databases, and services to accomplish tasks
  • Real-time Execution: Operating continuously with minimal latency for time-sensitive operations
